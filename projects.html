IoT-Enhanced Fish Farm Management and Automation System

Duration: 01/2024 - 04/2024

The IoT-based Fish Plant Monitoring and Automation System utilizes NodeMCU and Blynk to provide real-time monitoring and control of critical parameters such as water level, pH level, turbidity, temperature, and feeding schedules. Sensors connected to the NodeMCU capture data on these parameters, which is then transmitted to the Blynk app for remote monitoring and automated control. This system ensures optimal environmental conditions for fish health and growth, enhancing efficiency and sustainability in aquaculture operations.
